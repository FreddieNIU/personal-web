When to visit

Peak travel is May–Aug. The climate varies by region, but includes cold northern winters (Dec–Feb) and a rainy monsoon season (May–Oct) in the south. Key holidays include Chinese New Year (Jan/Feb, dates vary), the Lantern Festival (Feb/Mar, dates vary) and the Mid-Autumn Moon Festival (Sep/Oct, dates vary).
